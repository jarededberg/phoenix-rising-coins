Why people work with us

A friendlier way to sell coins

No walking into a shop with a shoebox and hoping for the best. Just send photos from home and hear what your coins are worth from someone local who actually collects.

Just take a photo

Snap a few pictures with your phone — front, back, and anything unusual. No cleaning, no appraisal appointment needed.

Get a fair, honest offer

What we're looking for

Copper, silver, gold, nickels & currency — any era, any condition

Copper

Wheat pennies, Indian Head cents, large cents, and old copper coinage from estate boxes or jar change.

Silver

Morgan & Peace dollars, pre-1965 dimes/quarters/halves, silver dollars, and "junk silver" collections.

Gold

Gold eagles, older gold coinage, and gold pieces passed down through family collections.

Nickels & Currency

Buffalo & Liberty nickels, silver war nickels, plus old paper money like silver and gold certificates.
