How It Works

Four simple steps, no shop visit required. Most people go from "found some old coins" to a real offer in under a week.

1

Take a few photos

Use your phone to photograph each coin (or the collection as a group) — front and back, in good light. Include anything unusual: dates, mint marks, or damage.

2

Send them our way

Fill out the contact form with your photos and a little info about what you have, or text/email us directly. It takes about two minutes.

3

We evaluate & respond

We look at metal content, condition, mint marks, and current collector demand, then reply within 1–2 business days with an honest, no-pressure offer.

4

You decide

Like the offer? We'll arrange a safe, convenient local meetup in the Phoenix area and pay you promptly. Not ready to sell? No hard feelings — no obligation either way.

Good to know

Tips for better photos

Use natural light

Daylight near a window shows true color and detail far better than flash, which can wash out silver and gold coins.

Shoot both sides

Dates, mint marks, and wear patterns on the back matter just as much as the front for an accurate evaluation.

Group similar coins

For jars of mixed change, a few overview photos plus close-ups of anything that looks old or unusual is perfect.
