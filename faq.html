Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you're probably wondering before you send over photos.

Is this actually free?

Yes. Sending photos and getting an offer costs nothing and comes with no obligation to sell. You only get paid — you never pay us.

How do you value my coins?

We look at metal content (copper, silver, or gold), condition, mint marks, date rarity, and current collector demand. For common "junk silver" or bullion-type coins, value tracks closely with metal spot prices. For rarer or collectible coins, numismatic value can matter more than melt value — we'll explain which applies to your coins.

Do I need to clean my coins first?

No — please don't. Cleaning can actually damage a coin's surface and lower its value to collectors. Send them exactly as you found them.

What if my coins aren't worth much?

That's okay. We'll always give you an honest read, even if it means telling you a coin is mostly sentimental or face value. No pressure to sell either way.

Do I have to meet in person?

Yes — we keep things local. Once you accept an offer, we'll arrange a safe, convenient in-person meetup in the Phoenix metro area to complete the sale.

How fast will I hear back?

Most submissions get a response within 1–2 business days. Larger collections may take a bit longer to review carefully.

What areas do you serve?

Phoenix, Scottsdale, Tempe, Mesa, Glendale, Chandler, and the surrounding Valley. We're a local, in-person business and don't currently accept coins from outside the area.

Can I sell just one coin, or does it need to be a whole collection?

Either is fine. We review everything from a single coin to a full estate collection.

Still have questions?

Reach out directly — we're happy to talk through what you've got.
